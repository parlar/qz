Notes:
- QZ default: pipelined chunked mode (2.5M reads/chunk), BSC adaptive no-LZP
- QZ ultra 3: 5M reads/chunk (2 parallel), BSC adaptive with LZP
  BWT thread cap raised to 16, OpenMP limited to 12 threads per BSC call
- SPRING: local reorder only 
- bzip2: single-threaded (1 core), hence slow wall time but low RAM
- pigz: parallel gzip (72 threads), fastest wall time but worst compression ratio

Changes since initial benchmark:
- Ultra 3: LZP restored for compress_adaptive_mt (helps order/rl streams)
  Compressed size improved 436.9 MB → 416 MB, speed 40.6s → 37.0s
- libbsc bwt.cpp: BWT encode thread cap raised from 8 → 16
- bsc.rs: OpenMP threads set to 12 for MT path (ultra/decompress_mt)
